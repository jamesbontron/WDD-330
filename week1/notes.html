Notes of Master Mobile UX Lesson 1

  • If you design for the smalles, least powerful device first, then you will focus on the content, and your core functionality.
  • The simple guidline is whatever you are doing, do mobile first.
  • Start small and scall up.
  • People don't know what they want until you show it to them.
  • Fast loading and functionality will make people stay in my website.
  • Accessibility is important. Place buttons, functions, menus or anything important where the user can easily reach them (depends on the device).
  • Reduce image usage (Or use SVG/Webfonts), optimize and minify CSS and JS Files.
  • GZIP files to eliminate unnecessary data.
  • You must provide the correct keyboard according to the type of input the user needs to fill in.
  • Arrange advertisements so that they do not occupy a large part of the screen and never use pop-up ads.
